*** Yoast SEO: Local Changelog ***

* update -  Sets the minimum supported WordPress version to 6.8.

* update -  Compatibility with PHP 8.4 and 8.5 is now being safeguarded more stringently
* update -  Bumps the minimum required Free version to 27.3.
